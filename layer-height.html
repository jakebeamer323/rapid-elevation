Layer Height Optimizer

Your Z-axis stepper moves in discrete steps. Printing at layer heights that aren't exact multiples of one full step forces the motor to hold a micro-stepped position — introducing Z-banding artifacts. This tool shows you the "magic numbers" that land perfectly on full steps.
