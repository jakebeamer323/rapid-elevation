Filament Diameter Variance

Inconsistent filament diameter causes uneven extrusion, surface rippling, and flow issues no slicer setting can fully compensate for. Enter caliper measurements taken at intervals across your spool to assess quality and get a flow adjustment suggestion.

Diameter measurements (mm)
