Common STL Issues
Issue Effect Fix
Non-manifold edges Slicer creates holes or missing walls Meshmixer Repair, Netfabb, or PrusaSlicer auto-repair
Inverted normals Slicer sees "inside-out" geometry Flip normals in Meshmixer or Blender
Degenerate triangles Zero-area triangles cause math errors Remesh or clean up in CAD software
Intersecting geometry Unpredictable slicing behavior Boolean union in CAD / Blender
Too many triangles Slow slicing, large file Decimate mesh in Meshmixer or Blender
